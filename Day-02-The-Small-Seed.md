# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 02

### 📚 Story Title
The Small Seed

---

## 📖 Story

There was a small seed in a garden.

The seed wanted to become a big tree.

Every day it looked at the tall trees and became sad.

It thought,

> "I am too small."

One day the sun said,

> "Don't compare yourself with the trees."

The rain said,

> "Just grow a little every day."

The seed listened.

It grew one leaf.

Then another.

Months passed.

Years passed.

One day, it became the tallest tree in the garden.

The tree smiled and said,

> "I became strong because I never stopped growing."

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### Q1. Who was the main character?

**Answer:**
The main character was a small seed.

---

### Q2. Why was the seed sad?

**Answer:**
The seed was sad because it was smaller than all the trees.

---

### Q3. Who helped the seed?

**Answer:**
The sun and the rain helped the seed.

---

### Q4. What happened at the end?

**Answer:**
The seed became the tallest tree in the garden.

---

### Q5. What lesson did you learn?

**Answer:**
Never compare yourself with others. Keep growing every day.

---

# 🌸 Bonus Challenge

### What did this story teach you?
