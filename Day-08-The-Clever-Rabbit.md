# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 08

### 📚 Story Title

**The Clever Rabbit**

---

# 📖 Story

Once upon a time, a lion lived in a forest.

He killed many animals every day.

All the animals became frightened.

One day, they made a plan.

They decided to send one animal to the lion every day.

One day, it was a clever rabbit's turn.

The rabbit reached the lion very late.

The angry lion asked,

*"Why are you late?"*

The rabbit replied,

*"Another lion stopped me on the way."*

The lion became furious.

The rabbit took him to a deep well.

The lion looked into the water and saw his own reflection.

Thinking it was another lion, he jumped into the well.

The lion drowned, and all the animals became safe.

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Who lived in the forest?

**Answer:**
A lion lived in the forest.

---

### 2. Why were the animals frightened?

**Answer:**
The lion killed many animals every day, so the animals were frightened.

---

### 3. What plan did the animals make?

**Answer:**
The animals decided to send one animal to the lion every day.

---

### 4. Why was the rabbit late?

**Answer:**
The rabbit said that another lion stopped him on the way.

---

### 5. How did the rabbit trick the lion?

**Answer:**
The rabbit showed the lion his own reflection in the well. The lion thought it was another lion and jumped into the well.

---

### 6. What is the moral of the story?

**Answer:**
Intelligence is more powerful than strength. We should never fear challenges.

---

# 🌟 Bonus Challenge

### Question

**Why is intelligence more powerful than strength?**

### My Answer

Intelligence is more powerful than strength because it helps us solve problems. A strong person can win with power, but an intelligent person can win with ideas. In the story, the rabbit defeated the lion using his intelligence, not his strength. This teaches us that a smart mind can overcome big challenges.

---

# 📖 Moral

**A clever mind can achieve what strength alone cannot.**
