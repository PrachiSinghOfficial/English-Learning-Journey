# 🇬🇧 English Practice - Day 10

## 📖 Story

### The Honest Farmer

A farmer worked hard every day in his fields.

One morning, while walking home, he found a small bag full of money.

He did not keep the money.

Instead, he gave the bag to the village head.

The real owner came and thanked the farmer.

The farmer felt proud because honesty is more valuable than money.

---

## 📚 Vocabulary

- Farmer
- Fields
- Reward
- Proud
- Honest
- Trust

---

## ❓ Questions & Answers

1. Who worked in the fields?
- A farmer.

2. What did the farmer find?
- He found a small bag full of money.

3. Did he keep the money?
- No, he did not.

4. Who thanked the farmer?
- The owner of the bag.

5. What is more valuable than money?
- Honesty.

---

## ⭐ Bonus Challenge

If I found a wallet on the road, I would return it because it does not belong to me. Honesty helps people trust each other, and trust is more valuable than money.

---

## 🎤 Speaking Practice

I always try to be honest. Honesty builds trust and respect. Money can be earned again, but trust is difficult to earn once it is lost. An honest person sleeps peacefully because they have nothing to hide.
