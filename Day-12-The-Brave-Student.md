# 🇬🇧 English Practice - Day 12

## 📖 Story

### The Brave Student

A college student dreamed of studying in another country.

Many people told her that it was impossible.

Instead of giving up, she studied every day.

She improved her English, learned new skills, and completed many projects.

Some days were difficult, but she never stopped trying.

One day, an opportunity came, and she was ready because she had worked hard for it.

---

## 📚 Vocabulary

- Dreamed
- Impossible
- Improved
- Opportunity
- Ready
- Worked Hard

---

## ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. What was the student's dream?

Her dream was to study in another country.

### 2. What did people tell her?

People told her that it was impossible.

### 3. What did she improve?

She improved her English, learned new skills, and completed many projects.

### 4. Did she give up?

No, she never gave up.

### 5. Why was she ready when the opportunity came?

She was ready because she had worked hard for it.
