# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 01

### 📚 Story Title
The Two Friends

---

## 📖 Story

Rahul and Aman were best friends.

One day, they went to a forest.

Suddenly, they saw a big bear coming toward them.

Rahul quickly climbed a tree.

Aman did not know how to climb.

He remembered that bears do not attack dead animals.

So, he lay down on the ground and pretended to be dead.

The bear came close, smelled Aman, and walked away.

After the bear left, Rahul came down and asked,

> "What did the bear whisper in your ear?"

Aman smiled and said,

> "The bear told me never to trust a friend who leaves you in danger."

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### Q1. Who were the two friends?

**Answer:**
The two friends were Rahul and Aman.

---

### Q2. Where did they go?

**Answer:**
They went to a forest.

---

### Q3. What animal did they see?

**Answer:**
They saw a bear.

---

### Q4. What did Rahul do?

**Answer:**
Rahul climbed a tree.

---

### Q5. How did Aman save himself?

**Answer:**
Aman pretended to be dead.

---

### Q6. What did the bear say to Aman?

**Answer:**
The bear told Aman never to trust a friend who leaves him in danger.

---

### Q7. What is the moral of the story?

**Answer:**
Never trust a friend who leaves you in danger.

---

# 🌸 Bonus Challenge

### What qualities should a good friend have?

**Answer:**

A good friend never leaves you in danger.

A true friend always stands by you in every situation.

They never break your trust.
