# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 04

### 📚 Story Title

**The Honest Woodcutter**

---

# 📖 Story

Once there was a poor woodcutter who lived near a river.

One day, while cutting wood, his axe accidentally fell into the river.

He became very sad because he could not afford to buy another axe.

Suddenly, a fairy appeared and asked him why he was crying.

The fairy first brought a golden axe.

The woodcutter said,

*"This is not my axe."*

Then the fairy brought a silver axe.

Again, the woodcutter said,

*"This is not my axe."*

Finally, the fairy brought his old iron axe.

The woodcutter smiled happily and said,

*"Yes, this is my axe."*

The fairy was pleased with his honesty and rewarded him with all three axes.

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Who was the main character?

**Answer:**
The main character was a woodcutter.

---

### 2. Where did the woodcutter live?

**Answer:**
He lived near a river.

---

### 3. What fell into the river?

**Answer:**
The woodcutter's axe fell into the river.

---

### 4. Who came to help him?

**Answer:**
A fairy came to help him.

---

### 5. Which axe belonged to the woodcutter?

**Answer:**
The old iron axe belonged to the woodcutter.

---

### 6. Why did the fairy reward him?

**Answer:**
The fairy rewarded him because he was honest.
