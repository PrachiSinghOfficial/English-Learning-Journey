# 🇬🇧 English Learning Journey

## Day 13 - Reading, Speaking & Writing Practice

### 📖 Reading Topic
**The Scholarship Dream**

Emma dreamed of studying abroad. Even though her English was not very good and she had no guide, she continued learning every day. She improved her skills, stayed consistent, and finally earned a scholarship. She learned that success comes from patience, consistency, and believing in yourself.

---

## 📝 Questions & Answers

### 1. What was Emma's dream?
To study in another country.

### 2. Why did she feel afraid?
Because her English was not good and she had no one to guide her.

### 3. What promise did she make to herself?
She promised herself that she would study every day and never give up.

### 4. What did she do every day?
She studied English, learned new computer skills, and worked on personal projects.

### 5. What lesson did Emma learn?
Consistency, patience, and believing in yourself can make dreams come true.

---

## ✍️ Writing Practice

### Topic
**One dream that I will never give up on**

### My Answer

One dream I will never give up on is becoming a better and independent person. I want to make myself proud by working hard every day. I believe success comes with consistency and patience. No matter how difficult life becomes, I will keep moving forward. My dream gives me hope and the courage to become the best version of myself.
