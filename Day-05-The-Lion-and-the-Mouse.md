# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 05

### 📚 Story Title

**The Lion and the Mouse**

---

# 📖 Story

One day, a lion was sleeping peacefully in the forest.

A little mouse was running around and accidentally ran over the lion's paw.

The lion woke up angrily and caught the mouse.

The mouse said,

*"Please let me go. One day I may help you."*

The lion laughed because the mouse was very small, but he decided to let it go.

A few days later, the lion was trapped in a hunter's net.

The mouse heard the lion roaring and quickly came to help.

It chewed the ropes with its sharp teeth and set the lion free.

The lion thanked the mouse and realized that even the smallest friend can be a great help.

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Who were the two main characters?

**Answer:**
The two main characters were the lion and the mouse.

---

### 2. Why did the lion catch the mouse?

**Answer:**
The lion caught the mouse because it accidentally ran over his paw.

---

### 3. What did the mouse ask the lion?

**Answer:**
The mouse asked the lion to let him go and said, "One day I may help you."

---

### 4. How did the mouse help the lion?

**Answer:**
The mouse chewed the ropes with its sharp teeth and freed the lion.

---

### 5. What did the lion learn at the end?

**Answer:**
The lion learned that even a small friend can give great help.

---

### 6. Where was the lion trapped?

**Answer:**
The lion was trapped in a hunter's net.

# 🌟 Bonus Challenge

### Question

**Can a small person make a big difference? Why?**

### My Answer

Yes, a small person can make a big difference. We all know about Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam. He belonged to a poor family. As a child, he sold newspapers by riding a bicycle, but he never gave up on his dream. Because of his hard work and determination, he achieved great success. Today, he is no longer with us, but the whole world remembers him as the **"Missile Man"** and one of India's greatest leaders.

---

# 📖 Moral

**Never underestimate anyone because even the smallest person can make a big difference.**
