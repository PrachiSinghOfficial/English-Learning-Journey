# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 07

### 📚 Story Title

**The Honest Shopkeeper**

---

# 📖 Story

One day, a young boy went to a small shop to buy some fruits.

The shopkeeper accidentally returned **₹500 extra** to the boy.

The boy noticed the mistake after reaching home.

The next morning, he went back to the shop and returned the extra money.

The shopkeeper smiled and thanked the boy for his honesty.

He said,

*"Honesty is more valuable than money."*

The boy felt happy because he had done the right thing.

# 🌟 Bonus Challenge

### Question

**Why is honesty important in life?**

### My Answer

Honesty makes a person valuable. I remember the story of the woodcutter. If the woodcutter had not shown his honesty to the fairy, she would never have given him the gold and silver axes. Honest people may not become rich immediately, but they earn respect and trust. That is why honesty is one of the best qualities a person can have.

---

# 📖 Moral

**Honesty earns respect, trust, and true success.**
