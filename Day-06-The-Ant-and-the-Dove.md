# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 06

### 📚 Story Title

**The Ant and the Dove**

---

# 📖 Story

One hot summer day, an ant was very thirsty.

It went to a river to drink water.

Suddenly, the ant slipped and fell into the river.

It started drowning and cried for help.

A dove sitting on a tree saw the ant.

The dove quickly dropped a leaf into the water.

The ant climbed onto the leaf and reached the riverbank safely.

A few days later, a hunter came to catch the dove.

The ant saw the hunter and bit his foot.

The hunter cried out in pain and missed his shot.

The dove flew away safely.

Both the ant and the dove helped each other.

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Why did the ant go to the river?

**Answer:**
The ant went to the river to drink water.

---

### 2. What happened to the ant?

**Answer:**
The ant slipped and fell into the river.

---

### 3. Who helped the ant?

**Answer:**
The dove helped the ant.

---

### 4. How did the dove save the ant?

**Answer:**
The dove dropped a leaf into the water. The ant climbed onto the leaf and reached the riverbank safely.

---

### 5. How did the ant help the dove?

**Answer:**
The ant bit the hunter's foot.

---

### 6. What is the moral of the story?

**Answer:**
Helping each other makes the world a better place.

# 🌟 Bonus Challenge

### Question

**Why should we help others?**

### My Answer

We should help others because one day we may also need help. Helping people makes us kind and respectful. A small act of kindness can change someone's life. When we help others, we make the world a better place.
