# 🇬🇧 English Practice - Day 11

## 📖 Story

### The Lost Puppy

A little girl was walking home from school.

She saw a lost puppy sitting by the roadside.

The puppy looked hungry and scared.

She gave it some water and food.

Then she called the animal shelter.

Soon, the puppy's owner arrived and thanked the girl for her kindness.

The girl smiled because helping others made her happy.

---

## 📚 Vocabulary

- Puppy
- Hungry
- Scared
- Shelter
- Kindness
- Arrive

---

## ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Who was walking home from school?

A little girl was walking home from school.

### 2. What did the girl see?

She saw a lost puppy sitting by the roadside.

### 3. How did the puppy look?

The puppy looked hungry and scared.

### 4. Who did the girl call?

She called the animal shelter.

### 5. Why was the girl happy?

She was happy because she helped the lost puppy.

---

## ⭐ Bonus Challenge

Kindness is important because it can save someone's life.

When we help others, we also feel happy.

---

## 🎤 Speaking Practice

Helping others makes us better people.

Kindness does not cost anything, but it can change someone's day.

A small act of kindness can bring a big smile.
