# 📚 Story Title

**The Honest Milkman**

---

# 📖 Story

There was a milkman in a small village.

He sold fresh milk every morning.

One day, he found a wallet on the road.

The wallet was full of money.

He looked around but could not find the owner.

So, he took the wallet to the police station.

Later, the owner came and thanked the milkman.

The owner gave him a reward, but the milkman accepted only a small gift.

Everyone in the village respected him for his honesty.

**Answer:**
Honest people can achieve anything. Honesty helps people earn respect and trust.

# 🌟 Bonus Challenge

## Question

**Why should we always tell the truth?**

## My Answer

We should always tell the truth because people who lie always live with fear. If you never tell a lie, you do not have to be afraid of anyone. You can stand in front of everyone with confidence, respect, and without fear. Truth makes a person strong and trustworthy.

**Truth and honesty always earn respect.**
