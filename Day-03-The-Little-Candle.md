# 📖 English Learning Journey

## Day 03

### 📚 Story Title

**The Little Candle**

---

# 📖 Story

Once upon a time, there was a little candle in a dark room.

The candle felt sad because it was very small. It looked at the big lamp and thought, "I am not important."

One night, the electricity went out. The room became completely dark.

A girl lit the little candle. Its small light filled the room and helped everyone see.

The candle smiled and realized that even a small light can make a big difference.

---

# ❓ Questions & Answers

### 1. Who was in the dark room?

**Answer:**
A little candle was in the dark room.

---

### 2. Why was the candle sad?

**Answer:**
It felt sad because it was small.

---

### 3. What happened one night?

**Answer:**
The electricity went out.

---

### 4. Who lit the candle?

**Answer:**
A girl lit the candle.

---

### 5. What did the candle learn?

**Answer:**
A small light can make a big difference.

| Important | Valuable or meaningful |
| Difference | A positive change |
